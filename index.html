Biography

I am a senior actuarial analyst at Hiscox. I currently work within the group reserving team focusing on US liability classes of business. Outside of this role, I am interested in software development, which in the work context would generally imply using R or SQL. As a challenge (or perhaps for a distraction) I decided to build this website, partially to archive some personal thoughts as well as using this as an opportunity to develop some new skills. It also serves as a showcase for some of my terrible creations.

This is my personal website and is not associated with Hiscox. As such all views and opinions expressed are my own and do not reflect those of my employer.

Ten music albums

A selection of albums that influenced my taste in music

Last updated on 4 May, 2020 1 min read Personal
Austerity - David Garbiak

Over on Facebook I’ve been nominated by my good friend Ashley Carter to choose ten albums that have greatly influenced my taste in music. One album per day, ten consecutive days. No reviews, no explanations, just the cover shot.

So here are the ten as I am too lazy to post consecutively on Facebook:

  1. Pendulum - Hold Your Colour

  2. Massive Attack - Mezzanine

  3. Tiësto - In Search of Sunrise 7 (Asia)

  4. Vangelis - Blade Runner Soundtrack

  5. Hybrid - Wider Angle

  6. MC Solaar - Cinquième As

  7. Bensley - Muskoka

  8. Rage Against The Machine - Rage Against The Machine

  9. The Prodigy - The Fat of the Land

  10. London Eleckricity - Syncopated City
